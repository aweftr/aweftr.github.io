Biography:

Yunlong Cheng earned the B.E. degree in Computer Science and Technology from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China, in 2021. Presently, he is pursuing a PhD in Computer Science and Technology at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, under the supervision of Prof. Xiaofeng Gao. His research interest focuses on data mining, prediction, and scheduling algorithms within the context of cloud services. Presently, he aims to optimize resource efficiency in the cloud while ensuring user satisfaction using machine learning and algorithmic techniques.

Research Interests:

Cloud Computing, Data Mining, Prediction, Scheduling Algorithms.
